Partner Points

Partner Points are purchased from the team at a rate determined by the team. Once partner purchases points, they can use those points as fan incentives and to convert sales leads for their products or services. The revenue generated from selling points to partners can be built into integrated packages being pitched to prospective partners, or sold as an upsell to gain incremental revenue from an existing deal.
